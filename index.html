About Us

Summer Town Real Estate Company was established on November 22, 2020, in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, as a limited liability company. Since its launch, the company has succeeded in providing innovative real estate solutions that combine luxury and sustainability, meeting the aspirations of clients seeking the highest standards of quality and excellence in various real estate, hospitality, and entertainment sectors.

What Sets Us Apart ?

Experience a world of luxury through our exclusive brands in hospitality, retail, and entertainment — where every detail is designed to impress. From stunning interiors to iconic exteriors, we craft spaces that captivate the elite. With a strong commitment to sustainability, we deliver elegance with a conscience

Mrs. Zeina Said Abdullah Al-Sayed is the founder and managing director of Summertown Real Estate. With her strategic vision and leadership, she has established the company as a respected name in the industry, known for innovation, quality.
