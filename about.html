Sheikh Saeed bin Abdullah Al-Sayed

(May Allah have mercy on him)

Sheikh Saeed bin Abdullah Al-Sayed was a pioneer in the fields of trade and investment in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. He began his business journey in 1965 by establishing "Saeed Trading, Import and Contracting Establishment" in Riyadh, which later evolved into "Al-Sayed Sons Co. Ltd." Known for his visionary leadership and strong Islamic values, Sheikh Saeed was also a prominent supporter of charitable and humanitarian work. Today, his legacy lives on through his sons, who continue to lead a diverse portfolio of real estate, commercial, and philanthropic ventures that contribute to economic growth and community service.
