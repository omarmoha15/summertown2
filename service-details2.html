The Rise

Discover unparalleled luxury and modern elegance at The Rise. This exclusive project encompasses 7,300 sqm of thoughtfully designed space, offering 15 floors of sophisticated hotel apartments. Perfectly suited for both short-term and long-term stays, The Rise sets a new standard in upscale urban living. Situated in a prime location, the development also features an upscale retail space on the ground floor, adding convenience and vibrancy to the surrounding area. Whether you're a traveler or a resident, The Rise is designed to provide the ultimate comfort and luxury experience.
